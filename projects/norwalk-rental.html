Norwalk Lane

LIVE

A direct-booking site for one premium short-term rental. Every platform booking rents you a guest and charges for the introduction — this site keeps the relationship, the margin, and the returning guest's email.

norwalklane.com

about

Airbnb keeps the relationship, the fees, and the rules. For a single premium property, that trade is worse than it looks — so this is the counter-move: a fast, boutique page where professional photography does the selling and guests book direct. No listing template, no platform fee, and the repeat guest emails you.

Different muscle from the SaaS in this directory — hospitality copy, art direction, a checkout with exactly one product. Which is the point. The shipping instinct is supposed to generalize.
