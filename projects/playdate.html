PlayDate Live

Make the playdate happen — without the awkward texts. A friendly, no-pressure invite you send as a link.

playdate.site
About This Project

Texting a parent you barely know to suggest a playdate is quietly excruciating — what if they say no, what if they ghost? So the playdate often just doesn't happen. PlayDate replaces that cold-open text with a warm little artifact: pick a few dates, add the park, note the allergies and whether siblings are welcome, and send a link. The other parent taps a date, adds a note, done. No accounts, no apps, no friction — thirty seconds end to end.

It's the smallest product in this index and it still carries the house thesis: the invite link is a context layer. All the details that would take six awkward follow-up texts — dates, spot, food stuff, siblings — arrive up front, so nobody starts from a blank slate.
