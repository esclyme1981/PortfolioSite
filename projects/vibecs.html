VibeCS Live · Paying Customers

Zero-config AI customer support. Paste your URL, embed one script tag, and a support agent that already knows your product is live in under three minutes.

cs-vibe.com
The Problem

Intercom and Zendesk are built for companies with a support team — and a week to spend on setup, training data, and prompt configuration. Indie builders and small SaaS operators have none of that. So their sites either have no support at all, or a generic chatbot that cheerfully hallucinates answers about a product it's never read.

The people who most need automated support are exactly the people who will never configure it. The setup step isn't a hurdle — it's the whole reason the category doesn't serve them.

What I Built

VibeCS deletes the setup step. You paste your URL; a crawler reads your landing page, docs, FAQs, and pricing; and the widget goes live with one async script tag. The agent's knowledge base is your website — nothing to write, nothing to train, re-crawled weekly so it stays current.

  • Closed-loop by design The agent answers only from crawled content, and says "I don't know" rather than guessing. Hallucination isn't mitigated — it's structurally impossible to answer from outside the loop. That's a trust decision, not a technical limitation.
  • Personas instead of prompt engineering Two pre-built voices — "The Buddy" (friendly) and "The Engineer" (technical) — so operators pick a personality instead of writing a system prompt.
  • The widget is the funnel Every embedded widget carries a small "Powered by VibeCS" mark. The product's own footprint is its distribution flywheel.
  • It built its own sales team A fully automated outreach pipeline runs nightly: six directory scrapers find newly launched products, a detector skips anyone who already has support chat, and a three-layer email-discovery stack finds the founder. One recent run: 120 products scraped, 23 personalized emails sent, zero failures.
Stack
Ingestion
Auto-crawler over landing, docs, FAQ, and pricing pages; weekly re-crawl
Backend

AI
Anthropic Claude API, grounded in the closed-loop knowledge base
Widget
Async embed script — no impact on host-page performance
Growth
Nightly cron outreach pipeline with dedup, bounce handling, and prospect tracking in Postgres
Status & Outcome

Live, with real revenue. VibeCS has paying customers — acquired through the outreach pipeline it runs on itself. It's also dogfooded across this portfolio: the VibeCaddy site runs the widget, and BlockRate's did too.

Pricing is deliberately indie-friendly: free to test, $8/month unlimited, or $50 lifetime.

Lessons
The customers worth winning are builders and operators paying for leverage — not consumers hunting discounts. The pattern that BlockRate taught and VibeCS confirmed

VibeCS is the portfolio thesis at its most literal: a context layer (your own website) that eliminates a blank slate (an untrained support bot). When the setup cost of a category is the barrier, deleting setup is the product.
