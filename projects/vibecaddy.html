VibeCaddy

LIVE

Your AI tools forget everything. VibeCaddy doesn't — a Chrome extension that intercepts your prompt and prepends your project's stack, decisions, and conventions before it ever leaves the keyboard.

vibecaddy.ai

problem

Every AI session starts from zero. Builders working across ChatGPT, Claude, Lovable, and Bolt re-type the same paragraph — stack, conventions, decisions already made — dozens of times a day. Skip it and you get generic answers that ignore all of it. The tools have no memory of your project; the tax lands on you, one blank prompt at a time.

built

  • Interception, not copy-paste. The extension catches your prompt pre-send and prepends relevant context from VibeOS, the persistent memory layer underneath. Five vague words leave as a fully-contextualized instruction.
  • Six-plus platforms. ChatGPT, Claude, Lovable, Bolt fully supported; Base44 and Emergent in beta. One memory, every tool.
  • Relevance with guardrails. Context is retrieved per-prompt with cross-project pollution guards — dating-app facts never leak into fintech prompts.
  • A production eval agent. Nightly: score the day's optimizations, adjust retrieval thresholds, stale out decayed facts. A self-tuning loop most teams talk about and never ship.
  • Priced like a tool. Free tier · $8/mo · $120 lifetime. Indie tools for indie builders.

status

Live in the Chrome Web Store and dogfooded on everything I build. The honest read: resonance is real — nobody enjoys re-explaining their project — but distribution is throttled by Chrome's trust warnings on new extensions.

That's a distribution problem, not a product problem. Knowing which one you have is the PM job.
