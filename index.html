About The Operating Thesis
¶

Most product managers hand a spec to someone else. I write the spec, then I open the editor. Nine products in this ledger — iOS apps, Chrome extensions, SaaS — every one designed, built, and shipped by my own hand.

They all share one thesis: context layers that eliminate blank slates. VibeCaddy loads your project into every AI conversation. VibeCS turns your website into a support agent that already knows your product. Send Remy's agents do the getting-to-know-you before you ever say hello. WorkspaceHub replaces the blank PRD with a living prototype. Different markets, same conviction: software should show up already knowing the context.

The day job: I lead mobile engineering at SimplePractice. The scar tissue: five years founding Timebomb, a mobile social startup I grew to a team of fifteen. The proof of instinct: a #1 paid App Store app, internationally. And the discipline this portfolio is proudest of — killing the most sophisticated thing I ever built because it was the wrong business, no matter how good the engineering was.
