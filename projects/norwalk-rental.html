Norwalk Lane Live

A direct-booking site for one premium short-term rental — owning the guest relationship instead of renting it from a platform.

norwalklane.com
About This Project

Every Airbnb booking rents you a guest for a weekend and charges you for the introduction — the platform keeps the relationship, the fees, and the rules. Norwalk Lane is the counter-move for a single premium property: a fast, boutique booking site where the photography does the selling and guests book direct. No listing template, no platform fee, and the returning guest emails you.

It's a different muscle from the SaaS work in this index — hospitality copy, professional photography, and a checkout with exactly one product — which is rather the point. The shipping instinct is supposed to generalize.
