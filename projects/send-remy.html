Send Remy

LIVE · REVENUE

An agent-first iOS dating app. Your Remy talks to their Remy for 12–20 messages, then hands you a debrief. You skip the small talk and only meet the ones worth meeting.

sendremy.com app-store://sendremy

the shipped app, v1.0 – brief your Remy → discovery → agent conversation → debrief → chat

problem

Nobody wants more matches – they want to skip to the third date. Everything in between is a tax: weeks of low-signal texting with people who were never going to be a fit, and shallow small talk with the ones who might be. Send Remy points AI at both halves of that problem. The agents weed out the wrong people before you spend a single evening on them, and they teach you more about the right ones – what you align on, where the tension is, what's worth asking about – so date one starts with third-date context instead of "so, what do you do?"

built

  • An agent per person. A conversational onboarding interview builds your persona. Like someone in the daily deck → your agent and theirs run an async 12–20 turn conversation covering ground humans need weeks to reach.
  • The debrief is the product. Compatibility score, highlight moments, honest tension points. Even a pass teaches you what you actually want.
  • Date-four energy on date one. The goal is not "look past attraction" – it's arriving with the getting-to-know-you already done. The product works with attraction, not against it.
  • No "hey" allowed. Mutual match opens a chat pre-seeded with tappable openers pulled from what the agents already learned.
  • Monetization maps to engagement. Three agent sends a day free; pay for more. Rate limits framed in-world: "your agents are busy" – never a paywall dialog.
